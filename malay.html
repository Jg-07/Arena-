AOE-II Strats

(MALAY) 26+2 Siege Monk Rush (Smush)


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~12:45 (26+2 uptime w/ Malay Faster Age Up bonus).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar, start pushing in Deer (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then a Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 to Berries (14/20)
  • 4 to Boar, build 2 Houses when you hit 18 population (18/20)
  • 3 to Berries (21/30)
  • 3 to Wood, for 7 in total (24/30)
  • 2 to Gold, and move 1 from under the Twon Center to Gold for 3 total (26/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Send 1 more Villager from under the Town Center to Gold for 4 total
  • Build Market + Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best). When the Market is built, sell your 200 Stone and buy 100 Food.
  • 2 Villagers to Gold (28/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Have 5 Villagers on Food
  • Send 4 Villagers from under the Town Center and 2 villagers from Berries to Gold, for 11 total.
  • Send 3 Villagers from under the Town Center to Wood.
  • 2 Villagers from Gold should build a Monastery and Siege Workshop either at home or forard, depending on the distance between you and the opponent.
