AOE-II Strats

(KHMER) 23+0 3 Town Center Boom


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~12:45 (23+0 uptime with Khmer, more generically 21+2 uptime)


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Berries (11/15)
  • Lure Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then send to straggler trees (13/15)
  • 1 more Villager to Boar, for 8 total (14/20)
  • 2 more to straggler trees (16/20)
  • Get second Boar, then start pushing in Deer
  • 1 more to stragler trees, for 4 total (18/20)
  • 1 build House, then send to Boar (18/20)
  • Get 100 Wood, then build a Lumber Camp with the 4 Villagers on straggler trees near a woodline
  • 2 more to huntables under the Town Center (20/25)
  • 3 to Gold (23/25)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


    CLICK CASTLE


  • On your way up, build at least 2 Farms, then send as many Villagers from under the Town Center to the woodline.
  • You can keep 3-4 vils on stragglers if worried about too much overcrowding.
  • Once in Castle Age, you CAN build 2 Town Centers immediately, but due to the lack of Farms, it will be difficult to keep them all working.
  • Opt instead to build 1 extra Town Center, build the Farms necessary to run 2 Town Centers (between 10-12 Farms), then build your 3rd.
