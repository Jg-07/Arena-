AOE-II Strats

Siege Monk Rush (Smush)


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~15:20 (27+2 uptime).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Beries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • 1 Villager builds 2 Houses (18/20)
  • 1 more to huntables under TC (19/30)
  • 1 Villager to woodline, for 5 total (20/30)
  • House Builder + 4 more Villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber Camp, for 5 total (24/30)
  • 1 Villager to Food near the TC (25/30)
  • 2 Villagers to Gold (27/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market & Blacksmith, with 2 on the Market and 1 on the Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 1 Villager to Gold + Loom (28/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • After clicking up, send 4-5 more Villagers to Gold
  • Send 2 Villagers forward
  • Once in Castle Age, drop a Moanstery and Siege Workshop near your opponent's base
  • If needed, buy some extra Wood at the Market to build a 2nd Monastery
  • Produce Monks, make a Battering Ram to break through the opponent's wall, keeping at least 1 Monk behind the Ram so the opponent cannot re-wall behind
  • One optional thing you can do with extra Monks is grab nearby Relics and put them inside your forward Monasteries
  • Once through, make more Monks, some Mangonels to pressure the opponent's buildings
