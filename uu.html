28+2 Castle Drop into Unique Unit


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~15:45 (28+2 uptime).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • 1 Villager builds 2 Houses (18/20)
  • 2 more to huntables under TC. Build 2 Farms (20/30)

    • Until the end of Dark Age, continue to add Farms every time you get 60 Wood until you have 6-7 built.

  • House Builder + 3 more Villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber camp, for 4 total
  • 1 Build another Farm (21/30)
  • 3 Villagers to Stone (26/30)
  • 2 Villagers to Gold (28/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market & Blacksmith, with 2 on the Market and 1 on the Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 2 Villagers to Stone(30/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Build your Castle with 4-5 Villagers
  • Organize your economy based on what resoures your Unique Unit costs.
  • Once you have the map control, forward a Vilalger to build a Siege Workshop for Siege to help push your opponent, alongside your Unique Units.
