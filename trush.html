AOE-II Strats

21 Population Tower Rush


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Feudal Age ~9:40 (21 pop + Loom)


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 3 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 more to Sheep, also lure Boar (11/15)
  • 1 more to Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 2 more to Berries (15/20)

    • Lure 2nd Boar

  • 1 more to berries (16/20)
  • 1 build House, then to Wood (18/20)
  • 1 more to Boar for 10 total. When done, take Food from 2 Sheep, 5 Villagers on each (19/25)
  • 1 more to Sheep (20/25)
  • 1 more to Wood (21/25)
  • Research Loom

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • On your way up, build a 2nd Lumber Camp and put 2 Villagers on it.
  • Put between 2-5 Villagers on Stone depending on how aggressively you want to perform your Tower Rush. If you just want a defensive reaction from the opponent, 2 works fine; if you want more serious damage, go for 4-5.
  • Forward 4-5 Villagers towards your opponent's base. Having your Scout guarding your Villagers is helpful, though not needed.
  • Upon hitting Feudal Age, immediately palce your first Tower foundation up against your opponent's wall. For the economy research Double-Bit Axe and Horse Collar.
