AOE-II Strats

(ETHIOPIANS) 22+2 3 Town Center Boom


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~13:10 (22+2 uptime)


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Berries (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then along with 5 more Villagers, collect Wood from straggler trees. When you get 100 Wood, build a Lumber Camp on a woodline. Lure your deer while doing this. (19/25)
  • 3 to Food sources underneath the Town Center (22/25)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market + Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 2 Villagers to Wood (24/25)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Once in Castle Age, build 2 Town Centers.
  • Ideally 1 should be on Wood and the other on Gold, but both can be built on Wood to help set up the Farm economy you need to boom more quickly before collecting Gold.
  • You capitalize on the extra Food you get from the Ethiopians economy bonus to help kickstart this boom
  • The low initial Farm count, however, means you will need to be heavier on Gold with your Town Center on Gold so you can buy Food at the Market so you can keep booming.
  • If done as efficiently as possible, a 21:xx or 22:xx Imperial Age Click time is possible, resulting in a 24:xx or 25:xx age time, useful for extremely fast pushes like Arbalest + Ram.
