AOE-II Strats

Basic Fast Castle 3 Town Center Boom


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~15:20 (27+2 uptime)


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 3 Wood (10/15)
  • 2 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Wood (14/20)
  • 2 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar. Build 1 Farm once Boar is in and also start pushing in Deer.
  • 1 more to Berries (17/20)
  • 1 build 2 Houses (18/20)
  • House builder + 3 more Villagers to a new woodline w/ new Lumber Camp (21/30)
  • Build 1 more Farm, for 2 total.
  • 3 Villagers to Stone (24/30)
  • 3 Villagers to Gold (27/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Barracks on the way up.
  • In Feudal Age, build Stable + Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 1 Villager to Stone + Loom.

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Send 1 Builder to Stone, for 5 total.
  • After grabbing the essential eco upgrades (Double-Bit Axe & Horse Collar), produce 1-2 extra Scouts and 1-2 Spearmen. Your Scouts will become Light Cavalry the moment you hit Castle Age.
  • Take off 4 or more Villagers from Wood to make Farms.
  • Forward 6 or more Villagers towards the opponent.
  • When in Castle Age, immediately drop the forward Castle, with your military close by to guard.
  • Once the Castle is up, shoot down the Wall while producing Janissaries.
  • Siege Workshop and Siege is helpful, but not necessarily needed unless the opponent internally walls and/or has a ranged army to defend.
