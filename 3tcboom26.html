AOE-II Strats

26+2 3 Town Center Boom


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~14:50 (26+2 uptime).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • 1 Villager builds 2 Houses (18/20)
  • House builder + 1 more Villager to huntables under the TC (19/30)
  • 5 Villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber Camp (24/30)
  • 2 Villagers to Gold (26/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market & Blacksmith, with 2 on the Market and 1 on the Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 2 Villagers to Wood, 1 on each woodline (30/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Once in Castle Age, build 2 Town Centers.
  • Ideally 1 should be on Wood and the other on Gold, but both can be built on Wood to help set up the Far meconomy you need to boom more quickly before collecting Gold.
