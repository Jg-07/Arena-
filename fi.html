AOE-II Strats

Fast Imperial Monks + Trebuchet Push


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age by ~16:30 (30+2 uptime) and in Imperial Age by ~21:30


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • 1 Villager builds 3 Houses, then go take Food underneath the TC (18/20)
  • 1 more Villager to huntables under TC (19/35)
  • 4 Villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber Camp (23/35)
  • 3 to Stone (26/35)
  • 4 Villagers to Gold (30/35)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market + Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 1 Villager to Stone, 1 Villager to Gold

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • When the Berries run out, send these 4 Villagers to take Gold.
  • Research Wheelbarrow and build your Castle with 4 Villagers.
  • 2 Villagers to Wood
  • If short on Food for Imperial Age, sell off excess Wood at the Market and buy Food. Click to Imperial Age once the Castle is up.

  • CLICK IMPERIAL

  • Prioritize Block Printing for your Monks and a Trebuchet as soon as Imperial Age is reached. These are your 2 biggest advantages.
