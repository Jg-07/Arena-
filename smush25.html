AOE-II Strats

25+2 Siege Monk Rush (Smush)


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~14:25 (25+2 uptime)


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House, then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • Start pushing in Deer
  • 1 Villager builds 2 Houses (18/20)
  • House builder + 1 more Villager to huntables under the TC (19/30)
  • 4 villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber Camp (23/30)
  • 2 Villagers + transfer 1 Villager from under the TC to Gold (25/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market + Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 1 Villager to Gold + Loom (26/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • After clicking up, send 2-5 more Villagers to Gold.
  • Send 2 Villagers forward
  • Once in Castle Age drop a Monastery and Siege Workshop near your opponent’s base.
  • Buy 100 extra Wood at the Market to help build a 2nd Monastery.
  • Produce Monks, make a Battering Ram to break through the opponent’s wall, keeping at least 1 Monk behind the Ram so the opponent cannot re-wall behind.
  • Once through, make more Monks, some Mangonels to pressure the opponent’s buildings.
