Civ-Specific Build Orders
AOE-II Strats

25+2 Castle Drop into a Wood & Gold Unique Unit


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~14:25 (25+2 uptime).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Berries (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House (13/15)
  • 1 more Villager to Boar, for 8 total (14/20)
  • House builder & 2 more Villagers to straggler trees (16/20)
  • Lure 2nd Boar
  • Start pushing in Deer
  • 1 more to stragglers for 4 total (17/20)
  • 1 build House (18/20)
  • 1 more Villager to Boar (19/20)
  • As soon as you have ~100 Wood, take the House Builder and 4 on straggler trees to build a Lumber Camp on a woodline.
  • 2 more Villagers to Wood, for 7 in total (21/25)
  • 2 more Villagers to Food sources under the TC (23/25)
  • 1 Villager to Stone + Transfer 2 from Food for 3 total (24/25)
  • 1 Villager to Gold + Transfer 2 from Food for 3 Total (25/25)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build a House on your way to Feudal Age
  • Build Market & Blacksmith, with 2 on the Market and 1 on the Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 1 Villager to Stone + Loom (26/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • On your way up, transfer 3 Villagers from near the Town Center, 1 to Stone and 2 to Gold, for 5 and 5, respectively.
  • Up to you whether or not you build your Castle defensively or offensively.
  • If you choose offensively, wait for enough wood for a Barracks and make 1-2 Spearmen to protect your Villagers as they go forward, then when you hit Castle Age, immediately drop the Castle near your opponent's base.
