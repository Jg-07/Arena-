AOE-II Strats

27+2 3 Town Center Boom


    *Executed correctly, this build order will have you in Castle Age ~15:20 (27+2 uptime).


  • 6 Sheep (7/15)
  • 4 Wood (11/15)
  • 1 Boar (12/15)
  • 1 build House then build Mill for Berries (13/15)
  • 1 Boar (14/20)
  • 3 more to Berries, also lure 2nd Boar (17/20)
  • 1 Villager builds 2 Houses (18/20)
  • 1 more to huntables under TC (19/30)
  • 1 Villager to woodline, for 5 total (20/30)
  • House Builder + 4 more Villagers to a 2nd woodline w/ new Lumber Camp, for 5 total (24/30)
  • 1 Villager to Food near the TC (25/30)
  • 2 Villagers to Gold (27/30)

    • CLICK FEUDAL


  • Build Market & Blacksmith, with 2 on the Market and 1 on the Blacksmith (Villagers from the woodlines are best)
  • 2 Villagers to Wood, 1 on each woodline (30/30)

    • CLICK CASTLE


  • Once in Castle Age, build 2 Town Centers.
  • Ideally 1 should be on Wood and the other on Gold, but bot hcan be built on Wood to help set up the Farm economy you need to boom more quickly before collecting Gold.
